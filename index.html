Paragraph tag, what is that?

The paragraph tag a HTML tag is used to declare / define a paragraph with text, details, info..etc. It may include an image or links, in general text.
To declare a paragraph tag, you should start with open tag with letter p in between and close with with. You can add as much as you like of styling to the P tag through the CSS; you may think about text color, font, line-height,... etc.
